Design Process

Empathize

Understanding, Before I start to designing a project. I will be spent most of the time to get know about the scope of the project and what the problem we are trying to solve.

Problem Solving

Solving The Problem, I will be starting to analyze the problem and gather some ideas at this stage. This stage includes market research, competitive analyses, consulting and exploring possible solutions.

Design

Designing Product, At this stage you’ll have a pixel perfect design for your Apps or Website. During the transition from wireframe into final product, I will create a prototype to simulate the product before development.

Development

Develop The Product, this is the final stage of creating a product. We starting to transfer the design into code. Before and after this stage, I will also iterate the process to makesure that the product is made as expected and achieve the goals.
